## Executive Summary
This Project Analyze on Synthetic Dataset containing 10000 orders across 
- multiple cities
- multiple restaurants
- multiple customers
- multiple delivery partners
- payment methods

The Objective is to identify business opportunities using SQL , POWER BI and Machine learning.

---

## Revenue Insights
- The Platform Generated 6 Million ₹ as Total Revenue from 10000 orders
- The Average Order Value is 622 ₹
- Company Commission contributes 20% from each orders
- High Value Orders significantly give more revenue

---

## Customer Insights
- Male and Female customers are nearly equally attributed
- Repeat Generate Customers generate high lifetime value
- A small group of customers contributes large amount of proportion
- High Spending Customers can be Targeted through premium and menbership programs

---

## Restaurants Insights
- certain cuisines performes outperforms other cuisines better
- some small restaurants create large amount of revenue by their quantity and quality
- Identifying low-quality restaurants to improve their quality

---

## Geographic Insights
- Revenue comes from multiple cities
- some cities have high cancellation orders while some have high delivered orders
- City Level Demand can help in optimizing campaigns and restaurant onboarding

---


# Operational Insights

- Most orders are successfully delivered with a relatively low cancellation rate.
- Delivery performance varies across cities.
- Experienced delivery partners contribute to operational efficiency.
- Monitoring delivery time helps improve customer satisfaction.

---

# Payment Insights

- UPI is the most frequently used payment method.
- Digital payments dominate overall transactions.
- Payment behavior can help design targeted promotional offers.

---

# Machine Learning Insights

A classification model was developed to predict order cancellations using customer, restaurant, order, and payment-related features.

Three algorithms were evaluated:

- Logistic Regression
- Decision Tree
- Random Forest

Because the dataset is synthetically generated, the models produced similar performance. In a production environment, richer behavioral data and historical customer activity would improve predictive performance.

# Business Recommendations

## Increase Customer Retention

- Introduce loyalty rewards for frequent customers.
- Offer personalized discounts to high-value customers.

## Improve Restaurant Performance

- Support low-rated restaurants through quality improvement initiatives.
- Promote top-performing restaurants during peak demand.

## Optimize Delivery Operations

- Assign experienced delivery partners to high-value orders.
- Continuously monitor delivery time by city.

## Reduce Order Cancellations

- Encourage prepaid digital payment methods.
- Identify high-risk orders using predictive analytics.
- Notify customers before order preparation for confirmation.

## Expand Revenue

- Increase restaurant partnerships in high-performing cities.
- Promote popular cuisines through targeted campaigns.
- Introduce premium membership plans for repeat customers.

# Conclusion

This project demonstrates how SQL, Python, Power BI, and Machine Learning can transform raw operational data into actionable business insights. The end-to-end workflow supports data-driven decision-making for customer engagement, restaurant management, delivery operations, and revenue optimization.
